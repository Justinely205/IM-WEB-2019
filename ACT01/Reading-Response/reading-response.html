What is Lialina's ideal internet user? Provide some of the qualities that she outlines.

Lialina's named her ideal internet user, The General Purpose User, Universal User and Turing Complete User, which was inspired by the Universal Machine by Alan Turing. It refers to a user that can accomplish their tasks regardless of what program they are given. Her Ideal user would be able to complete their task without a program specifically made for it. Some qualities she mentioned is informing the world about your activity without using a social media platform, which is made for sharing to the public. She uses the example of creating your own website to post about your breakfast in appose to your twitter feed, and your website homepage in appose to your twitter profile. Or logging into two account simultaneously by using two different browsers instead of using an existing app that can manage two accounts at once.
